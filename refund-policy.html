Refund Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

1. Overview

At JobDeJargon Pro, we strive to provide excellent service. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued for Pro subscriptions.

2. Pro Subscription Refunds

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new Pro subscribers:

  • Applies to first-time Pro subscribers only
  • Must be requested within 7 days of initial purchase
  • Full refund of the subscription fee
  • Access to Pro features will be revoked upon refund

Renewal Refunds

For subscription renewals:

  • No refunds for automatic renewals after the first billing period
  • Cancel before renewal date to avoid charges
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • You retain Pro access until the end of the paid period

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the 7-day guarantee period:

  1. Visit our Contact page
  2. Select "Refund Request" as the subject
  3. Include your account email and reason for refund
  4. We will process your request within 3-5 business days
  5. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are NOT eligible for refunds:

  • Partial month subscriptions
  • Subscriptions older than 7 days (first purchase only)
  • Renewed subscriptions
  • Downgraded accounts (Pro to Free)
  • Services already consumed or extensively used
  • Violation of Terms of Service

5. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your Pro subscription at any time:

  • Go to Settings â†’ Subscription
  • Click "Cancel Subscription"
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • You will not be charged for subsequent periods
  • Access to Pro features continues until the paid period ends

6. Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues that prevent you from using the Service:

  • Contact support immediately via our Contact page
  • We will work to resolve the issue within 48 hours
  • If we cannot resolve the issue, a pro-rated refund may be issued
  • Refund amount will be based on unused service time

7. Service Modifications

If we make significant changes to Pro features or pricing:

  • We will notify you at least 30 days in advance
  • You may cancel before changes take effect
  • If you disagree with changes, you may request a pro-rated refund

8. Refund Processing Time

Once your refund is approved:

  • Processing takes 3-5 business days from approval
  • Refund appears on your original payment method
  • Bank processing may take an additional 5-10 business days
  • You will receive an email confirmation when processed

9. Free Tier

The Free tier of JobDeJargon Pro:

  • Is completely free - no charges apply
  • No refunds necessary as there are no fees
  • Can be used indefinitely with limitations

10. Dispute Resolution

If you dispute a charge:

  • Contact us first before filing a chargeback
  • We will work with you to resolve the issue
  • Chargebacks may result in account suspension
  • We may provide transaction records to your bank

11. Special Circumstances

We may consider refunds outside this policy for:

  • Duplicate charges
  • Billing errors
  • Unauthorized charges
  • Extended service outages (our fault)
  • Medical or personal emergencies (case-by-case basis)

Each case will be reviewed individually. Contact our support team to discuss.

12. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

13. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

  • Email: billing@jobdejargon.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Us
  • Response time: Within 24-48 hours
