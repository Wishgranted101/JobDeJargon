Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

1. Introduction

JobDeJargon Pro ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password
  • Profile Data: Job title, industry, professional information
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely by third-party payment processors)
  • Communications: Messages you send to our support team

Information Generated Through Use:

  • Job Descriptions: Text you paste into our analyzer
  • Resume Data: Information you provide for resume generation
  • Generated Content: AI-created analyses, resumes, and cover letters
  • Dashboard Data: Saved jobs, application statuses, notes

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device
  • Analytics: Interaction patterns and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Process job analyses, generate documents, save data
  • Maintain Your Account: Authenticate users, manage subscriptions
  • Process Payments: Handle billing and subscription management
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop features
  • Communicate: Send service updates, respond to inquiries, provide support
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our Terms

4. AI Processing and Third-Party Services

We use Google's Gemini AI to process job descriptions and generate content. When you use our Service:

  • Job descriptions are sent to Gemini AI for analysis
  • Your resume data is processed to generate tailored content
  • Google may process this data according to their privacy policy
  • We do not control how third-party AI services use data
  • Avoid including sensitive personal information in job inputs

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data at rest is stored securely
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews and updates

Note: No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for different periods based on type:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account
  • Job Analyses: Until you delete them or close your account
  • Payment Records: As required by law (typically 7 years)
  • Usage Logs: Up to 90 days for analytics purposes

7. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

Service Providers:

  • AI Provider: Google Gemini for content generation
  • Payment Processor: Stripe for payment processing
  • Hosting: Vercel for website hosting
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (if implemented)

Legal Requirements:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To respond to legal requests from authorities
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • To prevent fraud or abuse

8. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Object: Opt out of certain data processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke previously given consent

To exercise these rights, visit your Settings page or contact us.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality
  • Authentication: Keep you logged in
  • Preferences: Remember your settings
  • Analytics: Understand how you use the Service

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit functionality.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

11. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers. We take steps to ensure your data receives adequate protection.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Note: We do NOT sell personal information.

13. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated via email.

15. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your privacy rights, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@jobdejargon.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Us

Your privacy matters to us.
