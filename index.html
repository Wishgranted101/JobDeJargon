Frequently Asked Questions

How does JobDeJargon Pro work? ‚ñº

Simply paste any job description into our analyzer. Our AI will translate the corporate jargon into plain language, highlight red flags, and give you an honest assessment of what the job really entails. You can choose different AI personalities to get the perspective that works best for you.

What are the daily limits for free users? ‚ñº

Free users can analyze 1 job description per day. This helps us manage API costs while still providing valuable analysis to job seekers. Pro users ($19.99/month) get unlimited analyses, plus resume and cover letter generation, document saves, company insights, and more.

What are the different AI personalities? ‚ñº

Brutal Truth Coach: No BS, tells it like it is with dark humor. HR Insider: Professional insights from the hiring side. Friendly Mentor: Supportive guidance without the snark. Pick whichever style resonates with you!

Can I save and export my resumes and cover letters? ‚ñº

Resume and Cover Letter generation is a Pro-only feature. Free users can analyze jobs once per day, but cannot generate tailored application documents. Pro users ($19.99/month) can generate unlimited resumes and cover letters, save them, and export as PDFs.

How accurate is the AI analysis? ‚ñº

Our AI is highly advanced at understanding context and language. However, AI is a tool to help you make decisions‚Äînot make them for you. Always review the analysis and use your own judgment. Think of it as a second opinion from a knowledgeable friend, not gospel truth.

What are the Pro company insights? ‚ñº

Pro users get access to company analysis, including ratings, culture scores, work-life balance indicators, pros/cons summaries, and salary ranges for specific positions. This helps you research companies before applying and make informed decisions about where to invest your time.

Is my data private and secure? ‚ñº

Yes. We take privacy seriously. Job descriptions you paste are processed by our AI to generate analyses but are not sold or shared with third parties. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime? ‚ñº

Absolutely! Cancel anytime from your account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, so you keep Pro access until then.

What happens to my saved jobs if I downgrade from Pro to Free? ‚ñº

Your saved jobs and analysis history remain accessible in your dashboard. However, you'll lose access to Pro-only features like the resume generator, cover letter generator, and PDF exports.

After downgrading, you'll return to the Free tier with 1 free job analysis per day. Any credits you purchased separately will remain in your account and can be used anytime.

Note: Your existing saved resumes and cover letters will be archived and can be accessed again if you upgrade back to Pro.
