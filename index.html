Frequently Asked Questions

How does JobDeJargon Pro work? ‚ñº

Simply paste any job description into our analyzer. Our AI (powered by Google Gemini) will translate the corporate jargon into plain language, highlight red flags, and give you an honest assessment of what the job really entails. You can choose different tones (Snarky, Professional, Formal) and AI personas to get the perspective that works best for you.

Is JobDeJargon Pro really free? ‚ñº

Yes! The Free tier gives you unlimited job analyses, access to all tones and personas, and a dashboard to track jobs (with account). The AI Resume and Cover Letter generators are Pro-only features. Pro users ($9.99/month) also get document saves, company insights, PDF exports, and 4 additional AI personas.

What's the difference between tones and personas? ‚ñº

Tones control the style of the response (Snarky = witty and blunt, Professional = balanced, Formal = diplomatic). Personas determine who's giving you advice (Brutally Honest Coach, Friendly Mentor, HR Insider, or Corporate Translator). Mix and match to get the perfect perspective for your needs!

Can I save and export my resumes and cover letters? ‚ñº

Resume and Cover Letter generation is a Pro-only feature. Free users can analyze jobs unlimited times, but cannot generate tailored application documents. Pro users ($9.99/month) can generate unlimited resumes and cover letters, save them, export as PDFs, and use a rich text editor for customization.

How accurate is the AI analysis? ‚ñº

Our AI uses Google's Gemini Pro model, which is highly advanced at understanding context and language. However, AI is a tool to help you make decisions‚Äînot make them for you. Always review the analysis and use your own judgment. Think of it as a second opinion from a knowledgeable friend, not gospel truth.
